Cover Song Generation

Here we show the results of our SongEcho alongside other methods for cover song generation. Listen to the audio outputs from each model and review the corresponding text tags and lyrics used for generation.

A good cover song should maintain vocal melody consistency with the original song, align the musical style with the provided text tags, ensure clear lyrics synchronized with the original song’s timing, and exhibit harmonious vocal-accompaniment integration with high audio quality.
